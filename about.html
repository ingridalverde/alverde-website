About Ingrid

I've been on both sides of this table.

As a parent, I know what it feels like to love a child with ADHD and not know how to help them. As a coach, I know what becomes possible when the right support arrives.

Alverde Coaching exists because I needed it once — and couldn't find it.

Work With Me

A different kind of foundation.

I spent 30 years in local government as a Director overseeing Economic Development, Communications, and IT. I coached business startups, led project teams, and learned that the most important work in any organization is helping people understand themselves well enough to move forward.

I was also raising three children with ADHD.

"You're not broken. Your brain just needs a different map."

Those two things — professional coaching and parenting children with ADHD — taught me more about this work than any single credential could. I know what it looks like when a young person has been told they're "not trying hard enough" for the thousandth time. I know what it feels like for a parent who loves their child deeply and can't reach them.

That's where Alverde comes from. Not a clinical framework. Not a one-size approach. A coaching practice built on the belief that clarity, connection, and confidence are available to every family — when you have the right guide.

How I work.

My coaching is strengths-first and practical. We don't dwell in the problem. We identify what's working, understand what's getting in the way, and build from there — with real tools, honest conversation, and a pace that respects your family's life.

Sessions are delivered online via video and supported by async communication between sessions — voice messages and email — so support doesn't disappear between calls.

Parents are not bystanders in this process. They're essential partners. Depending on the engagement, we work with the young person, the family together, or both.

Based in Petaluma. Working nationally.

I'm rooted in Sonoma County, Northern California — but Alverde Coaching serves families across the country, entirely online. Wherever you are, if your family is ready for something different, I'd love to talk.
